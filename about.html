Our story

Skincare rituals designed by a founder who lives them

Luxe Glow began in a Los Angeles treatment room where esthetician and entrepreneur Elise Harper partnered with modern women to repair stressed skin and restore glow. Today we deliver the same concierge-level care in every product and service.

Meet Elise Harper

Elise is a licensed master esthetician, product developer, and former tech marketer who built Luxe Glow to bridge clinical-grade results with luxurious daily rituals. Her approach blends ingredient expertise, business savvy, and radical empathy for women balancing big goals with skin sensitivities.

After scaling her treatment studio waitlist to 2,000+ clients, Elise developed our hero products to bring that transformation home. Today she leads product innovation, educational programming, and mentorship for the Luxe Glow esthetics collective.
