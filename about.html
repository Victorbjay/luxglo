Rooted in esthetician excellence

Built by Chicago-based esthetician and entrepreneur Noelle James, Luxe Glow honors skin diversity, clinical rigor and sustainable entrepreneurship.

Meet Noelle

The founder story

Noelle spent 12 years leading treatments at luxury spas and medical aesthetic clinics before founding Luxe Glow. Her mission: bridge clinical-grade performance with conscious sourcing and inclusive education.

After experiencing sensitivities caused by overly aggressive formulas, Noelle collaborated with cosmetic chemists to develop products that respect the skin barrier while delivering transformative results.
