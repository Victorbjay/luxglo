Privacy policy

We are committed to safeguarding your personal information and maintaining transparent practices across all Luxe Glow services.

Effective date: March 1, 2024

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as contact details, order history, consultation notes and payment information processed through encrypted gateways. We also collect technical data including device type, browser, IP address and site usage via cookies.

How we use your information

  • Process and fulfill orders, consultations and subscription services.
  • Provide personalized skincare recommendations and customer support.
  • Send marketing communications you opt into, with the ability to opt out anytime.
  • Monitor analytics to improve site performance and product development.

Data sharing

We share data only with trusted partners that help us operate our business, such as payment processors, shipping carriers and marketing platforms. These partners are contractually bound to protect your data. We never sell personal information.

Cookies & tracking

Cookies help personalize your experience and understand usage patterns. You can adjust cookie preferences in your browser or via our cookie banner when visiting the site.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may request access, correction or deletion of your personal data. Submit requests to privacy@luxeglow.com. We verify identity before processing requests.

Data retention

We retain customer data for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes. Transaction records are stored for at least seven years for accounting compliance.

Security practices

Our infrastructure uses SSL encryption, role-based access controls, regular vulnerability scans and PCI DSS compliant payment processing.

International data transfers

When transferring data outside your region, we implement appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

Updates to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes are posted here with a revised effective date. Material updates will also be emailed to subscribers.

Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, email privacy@luxeglow.com or write to: Luxe Glow Privacy Office, 2458 Fulton Market, Chicago, IL 60607.
