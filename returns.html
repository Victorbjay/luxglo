Support

Returns & Exchanges

Last updated: January 5, 2026

Luxe Glow is committed to your satisfaction. If a product does not meet expectations, please review the policy below and submit your request within the stated timelines.

Eligibility

  • Return requests must be submitted within 30 days of delivery.
  • Products must be at least 75% full and returned in original packaging.
  • Digital downloads, gift cards, and limited edition drops are final sale.

Process

  1. Email care@luxeglow.com with your order number and reason for return.
  2. Our concierge team will provide a prepaid label for eligible U.S. orders.
  3. Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days of receipt.

Damaged or incorrect items

Inspect your order upon arrival. If your item is damaged or incorrect, contact us within 7 days. Please include photos so we can quickly process a replacement or refund.

International returns

International customers are responsible for return shipping fees. Duties and taxes are non-refundable once collected by customs.

Exchanges

To swap a product, follow the return process above and place a new order for the desired item. Exchange credits can be issued for professional partners.

For additional assistance, email care@luxeglow.com or text +1 (213) 555-0198.
